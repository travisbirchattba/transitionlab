What founding partners receive

Three things that set this apart from standard engagements.

Direct facilitation

Every session is led by Travis personally — not delegated to a junior consultant or delivered through a recorded course. You get the full depth of 17 years of facilitation experience.

Tailored application

The program is adapted to your specific leadership context — the sectors you operate in, the pressures your leaders face, and the structural patterns unique to your organization.

Model influence

Founding partners help shape how the Lab evolves. Your experience, feedback, and insights directly inform what the program becomes for future cohorts.

Who this is not for

This is not a perk or a general development session.

Be honest with yourself about what your organization actually needs before starting a conversation.

Organizations looking for a one-day workshop or motivational speaker
Teams where leadership pressure is not yet affecting performance or retention
Organizations unwilling to examine structural conditions, not just individual behavior
Leadership teams not yet ready to experiment with how work is done
Who this is for

Organizations where waiting it out is no longer a viable strategy.

Pressure is already affecting retention, decision-making, or team stability
HR or executive leaders see the pattern forming and want to act before exit
The organization is willing to look at structure, not just support individuals
Leaders are open to redesigning how work actually flows through the organization
