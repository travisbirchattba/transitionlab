What this is

Transition Lab is a structured cohort program for managers, senior managers, and directors who are under sustained pressure, showing signs of burnout, and questioning how—or whether—to continue in their role.

Why organizations invest

  • Retain experienced leaders at risk of exit
  • Reduce burnout-driven turnover
  • Stabilize teams under pressure
  • Strengthen management capacity

The program

Leadership Transition Lab

  • 6–8 leaders per cohort
  • 8 facilitated sessions
  • Real-time workplace application
  • Practical experiments + reflection

Outcomes

For leaders

  • Regain clarity and direction
  • Reduce burnout and reactivity
  • Redesign how they lead day-to-day

For organizations

  • Retain key leaders
  • Improve team stability
  • Reduce hidden disengagement

The method

A structured cycle applied in real work conditions:

  • Awareness
  • Reframing
  • Experimentation
  • Integration

About

Led by Travis Birch—facilitator and former management consultant working at the intersection of leadership, transition, and practice-based development.
